    How to play

    Solo: guess the angle in 4 tries. An arrow says higher (↑) or lower (↓), with a warmth hint: Boiling within 5°, Hot within 10°, Getting hot within 20°, Warm within 50°, else Cold.

    Battle (2 to 8 players): one person creates a room and shares the code; everyone else joins. Each round you all see the same angle and lock in one guess. Points = how far off you were, so lower is better. Lowest total after all rounds wins.
